\section{Experiment 2: Multi-Model Quality Routing}
\label{sec:exp2}

\subsection{Setup}

Five model arms via OpenRouter: GPT-4o, Claude-Sonnet-4-5, Qwen-Plus, MiniMax-M1, GLM-4-Plus. Reward is quality-dominant: $R_t = 0.85 \cdot \text{acc}(a_t, x_t) - 0.15 \cdot c_{a_t}/c_{\max}$. Evaluated on 600 MMLU questions with 3 random seeds.

\textbf{RouteLLM-SW is excluded} --- it is architecturally binary (strong/weak) and cannot extend to $K > 2$ arms without $O(K^2)$ re-training. This structural gap is Moat~M2.
